ICN Bank

This is a Bank App project named ICN Bank App. The features of this project are:

  • You can log in as admin or as customer using the login page provided.
  • If you try to enter URL a root without being authantificated, you will be redirected to the login page.
  • If you don't have any accounts, you can register a new account. If the account is already existing, you have to use another emailId to register.

    • Customer choices

  • Press home and redirect to this page;
  • Press new account and redirect to a page where you can enter the balance that you want and wait until the admin will activate or suspend it. Until it will be activated, you can't do any transaction;
  • Press accounts and search you account detailed based on the id;
    • If your account is suspended, or created you will not be able to do any transactions; If your account is activated you will be able to do 3 types of transactions: "DEBIT", "CREDIT" or "TRANSFER";

    First two choices

  • If you choose the first one, if your current balance will be greater than the amount that you entered, the request will be send to admin, who will accept or decline the transaction. Until that moment the status will be pending.
  • If you choose the second one, the request will be send to admin, who will accept or decline the transaction. Until that moment the status will be pending.


    • The third one

  • If you choose the transfer, you will be able to to transfer money to another bank account based on the id of the account (even from your current account to another of your account). If your current balance is greater then the amount you entered, the request will be send to the administratod and he will decide if he will allow it or not. Until then, your transaction will figure as a DEBIT transaction from your current account, and to the account that you transacted the money, it will figure as a CREDIT transaction.

    • Admin choices

  • Go to Home and see this page
  • Go to Accounts and search for an account of your customers and see what transactions they did.
  • Go to Search Accounts. You will be able to search which customer do you want. You can even search some incomplete id, because it wil return all customers that contains in the email id that incomplete id.
    You can press the Accounts button from every customer and you will be redirected to a page where you can search only the bank account of that particular customer.
  • Or to go to requests. You will be able to see a table which is containing on every row the credit, debit, transfer and new account requests for every customer. You can also search for a particular customer if you want.

    • First two choice

  • If you press the "credit" button, you will be redirected to a page where you will see all the credit request that were made by that particular customer. You can choose to accept it or decline it. If you accept, then the status of the operation will be changed to ACCEPTED, and if you decline it, the status of the operation will be DECLINED and the money will be returned to the customer from the bank.
  • If you press the "debit" button, you will be redirected to a page where you will see all the debit request that were made by that particular customer. You can choose to accept it or decline it. If you accept, then the status of the operation will be changed to ACCEPTED, and if you decline it, the status of the operation will be DECLINED and the money will be returned to the bank.

    • Third choice

  • If you press the "transfer" button, you will be redirected to a page where you will see all transfer request that were made by that particular customer. You can choose to accept it or decline it.

    If you accept, then the status of the operation will be changed to ACCEPTED, and if you decline it, the status of the operation will be DECLINED and the money will be returned to the account of the customer where the transfer was happened, and from the other account, there will be charger the same amount and get back to the initial account from where the transfer began.




    • Last choice and testing

  • If you press the "new accounts" button, you will be redirected to a page where you will see all the credit request that were made by that particular customer. You can choose to accept it or suspend it.

    If you accept, then the status of the operation will be changed to ACCEPTED, and if you decline it, the status of the operation will be SUSPENDED and the customer can't make any more any transactions.
